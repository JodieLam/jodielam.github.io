Project Summary

bubble studio needed an avenue to advertise their services and build its brand and credibility. From previous competitor analysis, it was recommended that an Instagram Page would be best to meet the desired outcome. 

An instagram social media strategy was devised based on a competitor analysis to get a better understanding of what content prospective customers are looking for, what content would be best to promote their work and services, and what hashtags should go into the description. 

Graphics and videos were then created on Offeo, Figma and Adobe Photoshop as per the brand identity, and then published onto the social media platform.

The Process

01.

Instagram Page Set Up & Integrations

An Instagram Professional Page was set up for bubble studio. Integrations were also set up on the page to direct page visitors to external links, such as the pricing document, the website and their email. 

02.

Competitor Content Analysis

To receive an ABN and be eligible for a domain ending in .com.au, the business and business name was formally set up and certified. The registration details were then used to purchase the domain. 

03.

Graphic & Video Creation

The website design, content and copy was reviewed iteratively through feedback that was provided by test users. The design was then converted into code and developed.

04.

Insight Analysis & Improvement

The code and assets for the website were published onto the domain using cPanel. Basic SEO was also set up such as sitemap creation and connecting to the Google Search Console.
