Project Summary

bubble studio is a digital creative agency that specialises in helping small businesses build a digital presence. Their website is used to showcase their work to build trust with prospective customers, advertise their services and to provide a channel of communication for visitors to make any enquiries. 

A competitor analysis was conducted to ensure the prices and services are competitive and provide a point of differentiation. Mockups and copy were iteratively revised to ensure the content and design is compelling, accurate and relevant. 

The entire website was then designed and developed using the Mobirise application, then hosted on the domain using Go Daddy.

Business Establishment 

To get a .com.au domain, the business was formally registered, set up and certified by ASIC.

End to End Website Set Up 

Exposure to the back end server hosting, as well as the front end design and development of the website.

Brand Identity Creation

A brand identity was used to define the colours and typography used on the website.

The Process

01.

Business Plan & Competitor Analysis

A business plan was written to build the foundations for what the agency should offer. An analysis was also conducted to understand the market competition and how the  differentiate itself from others.

02.

Business Set Up & Registration

To receive an ABN and be eligible for a domain ending in .com.au, the business and business name was formally set up and certified. The registration details were then used to purchase the domain. 

03.

Prototyping & Testing

The website design, content and copy was reviewed iteratively through feedback that was provided by test users. The design was then converted into code and developed.

04.

Website Implementation

The code and assets for the website were published onto the domain using cPanel. Basic SEO was also set up such as sitemap creation and connecting to the Google Search Console.
