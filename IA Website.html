Interns Australia


Redesigning for clarity

The Interns Australia website is imperative for the organisation to provide resources and support to interns and employers. Their existing website was inconsistent and overloaded with information, making it difficult for their users to find information. To mitigate this, Jodie worked closely with the team to completely rebrand and redesign the website so the navigation was clear, and the content was useful and up to date. 

https://internsaustralia.org.au/ 


 The Process 

  1. ANALYSIS & RESEARCH - UX review of the current website to identify pain points and recommend improvements. Also conducted customer interviews with the team to understand what their requirements and what they would like in the revised website. 
  2. SYNTHESIS - Consolidation of data from initial analysis and interviews to scope the requirements for the website
  3. PROTOTYPE - Iterative designing with the team based on their feedback to ensure all requirements are satisfied and to ensure they are aligned with all changes
  4. PUBLICATION - The website was published and updated numerous times to reflect the changing requirements due to the downsizing of the business

Challenges

- Changing Requirements: During the project, there was a radical change in the service offering and size of the organisation. This required significant changes in the content and design of the final website. hanging the content and design of the website.
- Requirements Gathering: Customer interviews were conducted online and not in person as the team was spread out across different states. 
- Tool Constraints: The website design was limited to the functionality that is offered by Mobirise.

Learnings

- Collaborative Design: It is faster to co-design with the team, ideally in person. When working by myself, there were a lot more iterations and it took a lot longer to produce the deliverables.
- Frequent Checkins: Provide regular updates to the customer to share progress and to ensure that there are no changes to initial requirements.
